Goverment

Evidence:

People did not agree on how things were run.They wanted to have a say in how things were run in England.
One group in particular called the Parliamentarians believed that everyone should have say in how the country was ruled.
Unfortunately, King Charles I (the ruler at the time), disagreed with the idea as the government was an absolute monarchy.
According to dictionary.com, that means it is a government that is controlled by a king or queen who has all the power
and could do things without asking other people. He formed a party called the Royalists to fight against the
Parliamentarians. Unfortunately, the war went lasted from 1642 to 1651, which was 9 years
(nearly an entire decade). According to Wikipedia, in the end the Parliamentarians won, and King Charles
went on trial where he had faced charges with treason, and faced execution.
This war helped to abolish the absolute monarchy government type and introduce people have a
right to have say in how a government runs which made it a more democratic government, which
is a government where people have more say in certain laws or ideas.
This government is still around today with a government that is a constitutional democracy.

Reasoning
This was a big deal, because it showed that the king does not always have all the
power, and that people could have a say if they wanted to as they had started a
war to achieve that. It established the monarchy to a more democratic
government. Meaning people had more say in how things were run in the
government, and who should help run it. This government is still around today
running in a constitutional monarchy meaning that people get more power.

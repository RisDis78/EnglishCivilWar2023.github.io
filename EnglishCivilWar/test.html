Government

Evidence:
People did not agree on how things were run.
They wanted to have a say in how things were run in England.
One group in particular called the Parliamentarians believed that everyone should have a say in how the country was ruled.
Unfortunately, King Charles I (the ruler at the time), disagreed with the idea as the government was an absolute monarchy.
According to dictionary.com, that means it runs like a regular monarchy, but it gives the ruler in position more power.
He formed a party called the Royalists to fight against the Parliamentarians.
Unfortunately, the war lasted from 1642 to 1651, which was 9 years (nearly an entire decade).
According to Wikipedia, in the end, the Parliamentarians won, and King Charles went on trial where he had faced charges with treason, and faced execution.
This war helped to abolish the absolute monarchy government type and introduce people have a right to have a say in how a government runs which made it a more democratic government.
